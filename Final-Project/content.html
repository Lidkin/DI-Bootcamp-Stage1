Why Horror?

Fairy tales like Cinderella, Little Red Riding Hood, or Sleeping Beauty, heard in early childhood, always ended well. But a lot changed when I learned to read and began visiting the library independently. In the village where I grew up, there was no censorship of literature by age, so I could choose whatever I wanted. Turned out that the original fairy tales are not so cute at all ... And it was scary ... exciting! From then began a fascinating passion for the worlds of horror and surrealism: Lafcraft, Stoker, King, Lynch, and many others. I want to introduce you here to some of my favorite works. Enjoy!
